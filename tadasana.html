WNS Supplier Code of Conduct –

Setting the Ground Rules with Tadasana, the mountain pose.

WNS Supplier Code of Conduct is a charter that Outlines the governing principles of DOING THE RIGHT THING with highest ethical standards, in unison with the regulatory requirements and social commitments.

Just as the word 'Tadasana' is a combination of two Sanskrit words 'Tada' and 'Asana', ensuring the right balance strength, flexibility and endurance, the WNS supplier code of conduct defines the domain and the boundary within which we operate in honouring the regulatory, social and environmental responsibilities and commitment.

It is the framework basis on which actions and decisions are based.
