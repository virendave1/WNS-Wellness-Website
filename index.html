WNS Wellness - A Yogic Perspective Into Ethical Sourcing

At WNS, Ethical Sourcing, Sustainable Supply Management and Responsible Procurement Practices gears the organization towards a reliable, agile, and future-ready resource centre that is resilient, strong and flexible.

Using the analogy of Yoga, a traditional ascetic discipline that advocates, both mental and physical health to activate vital energy centers in the body, we take you on a journey to get you acquainted with our ethos of vendor partnerships and the uncanny resemblance it has with Yoga.
