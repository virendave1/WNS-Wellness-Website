Supplier Governance -

As fundamental as Pranayama, the breath control exercise.

Pranayam is known to have an universal appeal in regulating the key vitals of the body. The supplier governance process runs across the procurement function, to ensure highest ethical standards are adhered to in every aspect of supplier selection, onboarding, engagement and enrichment.

Apart from providing a sign off to WNS Supplier Code of Conduct, our suppliers are required to provide an annual confirmation and undertaking on their compliance standards, so that they join hands with WNS in pursuing their compliance journeys as PARTNERS IN PROGRESS.

This training program too is a part of the Supplier governance mechanism. We hope you found this module to be interesting and insightful to know about the WNS compliance framework.
