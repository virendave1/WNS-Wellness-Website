WNS Supplier Risk Screening –

Validating the key vitals with Setu Bandhasana, the bridge pose.

As a part of our KNOW YOUR BUSINESS PARTNER program, the credentials of the suppliers are verified through a risk screening process.

This is primarily to ensure that the compliance standards of WNS and those of the suppliers are in alignment and there are no prohibitions or restrictions, either through regulatory action or as trade prohibitions applicable to certain businesses / in certain territories.

WNS cares as much for the reputation of its suppliers and the risk screening also involves confirming any negative media reports about the supplier or any of its related businesses or activities.

Setu Bandhasana – as the very name suggests is meant to regulate the functioning of the various glands situated around the seven energy centers in the body. Our risk screening process also ensures that the key ingredients for a long term and a meaningful association with our suppliers are in place.
