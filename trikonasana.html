WNS Supplier Diversity and Sustainability -

As comprehensive as Trikonasana, the triangle pose.

Trikona, stands for three angles and this Triangle pose very much resonates with the cornerstone of the ESG framework – namely, Environment, Sustainability and Governance.

As a part of our commitment to the sustainability goals, all our operations are undertaken with utmost responsibility towards energy conservation and responsible usage of resources in managing our business. We also partner with our clients in their sustainability initiatives for minimising the carbon footprints. This responsibility and commitment flows back to our suppliers as well.

Through our Supplier Diversity Program, we monitor our procurement spend in a manner that we offer fair opportunity to small businesses and businesses of disadvantaged communities viz. Women, LGBT, disabled, military veterans, tribal communities, agrarian co-operatives etc.

As Trikonasana is meant to improve the agility and endurance of the body, our Sustainability and Diversity initiatives catalyse the resilience of the business in economic turbulence and uncertainty.
